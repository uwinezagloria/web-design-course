:: Our Values

We are a Christ-centered church committed to faith,
love, and biblical truth. Guided by God’s Word,
we seek to grow spiritually through prayer
, worship, and discipleship. We value community and fellowship,
supporting one another in love and unity.

:: Our Vision

"To be a Christ-centered community where
faith grows, love is shared, and lives are transformed
through the power of the Gospel."

:: Our Mission

We aspire to be a Christ-centered community where faith grows
, love is shared, and lives are transformed. Through worship,
discipleship, and service, we seek to bring people closer to God and
make a lasting impact through the Gospel.
